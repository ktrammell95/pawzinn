Our Facility

At Pawz Inn we offer over 4800 square feet of indoor cageless play area for your companions to have fun. We also have over 2500 square feet of completely fenced outdoor play area. We provide a range of activities to keep your companions completely stimulated while you are away. We provide toys, jungle gyms, and outdoor pools for those hot summer days. There are also plenty of quiet time areas for your companions to relax or take a nap. In addition we provide daily walks and playtime at no additional charge to you!

Meet the Owners

Pawz Inn is the heart and soul of both Tamee and Dennis, the owners/operators. They have fulfilled a life long dream by creating an environment for all dogs and cats that will keep them safe, active, and well taken care of the way YOU as their owner expects!

How can they say that, you may ask? Tamee and Dennis have, between them, eight different animal companions ranging from Chocolate Labs to Kittens.

They have a compassion for animals that is unwavering, and can identify with any pet owner that feels for their pets as they do. Pawz Inn offers you the piece of mind when you're not able to be with them, you can rest assured that your companions are having "a truly dog gone good time" and that they will be safe, happy, and comfortable.
